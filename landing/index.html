Core Features

Two Hotkeys. Zero Friction.

LingoKey is not a general translator or grammar checker. It is a workflow layer built specifically for developers who collaborate with AI coding assistants.

Optimize Before Writing

Turn Chinese or mixed Chinese-English drafts into natural, accurate English prompts. Preserve your technical intent without over-engineering the prompt.

  • Conservative & Enhanced modes
  • Diff highlighting for changes
  • One-click copy to clipboard

Explain After Reading

Select unfamiliar English expressions in any app and get a lightweight popup with Chinese meaning, technical context, and alternative phrasing.

  • Chinese meaning + tech context
  • Alternative expressions
  • Appears near your cursor
How It Works

Shorter Path, Same Intent

01

Press the Main Hotkey

A compact floating window appears. The input box is already focused. Just type your draft in Chinese or mixed English.

Ctrl + Shift + L
02

Get the Optimized Result

LingoKey rewrites your input into natural English. Changes are highlighted so you can see exactly what was improved.

03

Copy and Continue

Press Ctrl+C to copy the result, then paste it into Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, or any AI assistant you use.

04

Explain on Demand

Reading AI output and see an unfamiliar phrase? Select it and press the explain hotkey for instant context.

Ctrl + Shift + E

Built for Keyboard-First Developers

Every action is reachable without leaving the keyboard. Customize all shortcuts in the settings window.

Explain Window

Settings
